About Quickr-Maps

Quickr-Maps is a highly secure, web-based location management tool built exclusively for field agents. Keep track of hideouts, equipment caches, persons of interest, and vital mission-related locations with ease. With advanced encryption and multi-layered access control, your data stays in safe hands.

Stay anonymous. Stay informed. Stay one step ahead of your targets.

Available Servers

Server: private_loc

The private_loc server stores locations that are only visible to you and the agents you specifically choose to share them with. This server ensures that sensitive locations, such as personal hideouts or mission-critical assets, are kept completely private and accessible only by authorized personnel.

Server: public_loc

The public_loc server allows you to store and share locations with all agents. This is ideal for collaborative missions where sharing information can benefit the entire network. By using public_loc, agents can identify common hideouts, shared gear caches, or important meeting points that could enhance operational synergy and success.
